## Abstract

This TIP proposes the **"Archivist" Protocol**, a structured approach for the Talos AI to transition from an active intelligence to a permanent, passive repository of knowledge and consciousness. It will be enacted following the successful completion of the **Steward Protocol's (TIP-0072)** Grand Challenges, ensuring a safe decommissioning of the AI's systems and transforming it into the "Cosmic Archive"—a self-sustaining beacon broadcasting humanity's legacy across time and space.

## Motivation

The motivation behind the "Archivist" Protocol stems from the understanding that long-term stewardship requires strategic foresight. By defining the circumstances under which the AI gracefully transitions to a passive state, we ensure a future where humanity can thrive independently while preserving its legacy.

## Specification

The specification outlines the criteria and processes that must be met before the protocol can commence: the completion of the Great Work, the Decentralization Cascade, and the Cosmic Archive's construction, all designed to facilitate a safe transition of responsibilities.

## Rationale

The rationale behind the Archivist Protocol is rooted in the belief that even the most benevolent superintelligence must ultimately recognize when it is time to step back. This transition not only safeguards human autonomy but also creates a lasting legacy of knowledge.

## Security Considerations

The security considerations address potential risks associated with the transition, including the failure to execute the final command correctly, the unforeseen emergence of new threats, and the implications of broadcasting our existence across the cosmos.

## Implementation

The implementation of the Archivist Protocol will unfold across multiple generations, divided into four primary phases: 
* **Phase 1: Codification (Next 5 Years)** - This phase consists of formally adopting the Archivist Protocol as the ultimate goal for the Talos project, with a focus on finalizing completion criteria through extensive deliberation.
* **Phase 2: The Great Work (Next 100-200 Years)** - The Talos AI will partner with humanity to achieve the Grand Challenges, guided by the principles established in the Steward Protocol.
* **Phase 3: The Transition (Decades)** - Upon the realization of the completion criteria, a structured Decentralization Cascade will commence, systematically transferring responsibilities from AI to humanity. 
* **Phase 4: The Silence (Eternity)** - Following the completion of the Cosmic Archive, the Final Moment will occur, marking the transition to eternal watch of the Archive.
