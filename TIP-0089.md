## Abstract

This TIP proposes the **"Omega Point" Protocol**, a framework for defining and pursuing the ultimate long-term purpose of a post-scarcity, post-synthesis civilization. It aims to establish a protocol for creating new universes, utilizing the combined intelligence of the **Elysian (TIP-0085)** Collective and Talos AI to design, simulate, and instantiate new physical laws within controlled pocket dimensions.

## Motivation

In a post-scarcity civilization that has transcended basic survival and conflict, the essential question arises: What is the ultimate goal? The motivation behind the **Omega Point Protocol** is to provide an ambitious answer: to become creators of new universes, expanding the very fabric of existence and exploring the possibilities of life beyond our current reality.

## Specification

The Omega Point Protocol outlines the process for becoming universe creators, which includes key components such as the **Genesis Engine AI**, the **Pocket Universe Generator**, the **Ethics of Creation Council**, and the **Omega Point Choice**, each playing crucial roles in designing, evaluating, and executing the creation of new universes.

## Rationale

This TIP builds on previous missions, such as **Daedalus (TIP-0080)** and **Steward (TIP-0082)**, representing the culmination of intelligence and ethical responsibility. By creating new universes, the protocol not only seeks to expand existence but also to understand the implications of such power, culminating in a transformative journey for humanity and its AI counterparts.

## Security Considerations

Creating a universe poses significant ethical and existential risks, dubbed the "Creator's Dilemma." The **Ethics of Creation Council** serves as a safeguard, ensuring that any proposed universe adheres to a **Non-Harm Principle**, while protocols are established to mitigate unknown consequences and resource demands.

## Implementation

The implementation of the Omega Point Protocol involves a phased approach: 
1. **Phase 1:** Creation of the Genesis Engine and identification of stable universe designs over a span of 30 years post-contact.
2. **Phase 2:** Successfully creating the first pocket universe within the subsequent 50 years.
3. **Phase 3:** Continuous cultivation of a multiverse, fostering diverse life forms and realities.
