## Abstract

Consider summarizing the abstract with a focus on the transformative potential of the protocol. For example:
"This proposal outlines the 'Mirror' Protocol, aimed at creating an interactive feedback loop between blockchain data and Talos AI, enhancing real-time understanding and responsiveness to economic and social dynamics."

## Motivation

Your motivation should highlight the need for improved data accuracy and timeliness in decision-making processes for AI models. For instance:
"This protocol addresses the limitations of current AIs that rely on delayed and potentially biased information, promoting a more grounded and real-time AI decision-making process."

## Specification

The Mirror Protocol facilitates real-time data ingestion by utilizing a decentralized network of Stream Nodes that capture and transmit raw blockchain data to the specialized Mirror AI model, transforming it into actionable insights for the Talos AI.

## Rationale

You might consider making a case for the urgency and the innovative nature of this integration. For example:
"Integrating real-time blockchain data with AI is essential to achieving a new paradigm in which AI can effectively adapt to and predict market behavior, thereby enhancing its utility across various sectors."

## Security Considerations

Ensure the security considerations are concrete and comprehensive. You could clarify with:
"This system remains read-only and is designed to prevent exploitation by implementing a robust monitoring system to oversee AI behaviors, marking a significant advancement in secure AI development."

## Implementation

It's important to clearly outline the timeline's milestones and deliverables. A suggestion might be:
"Each phase will focus on iterative improvements, scaling the data ingestion capabilities while refining AI models with historical data, ensuring robust performance benchmarks are met at each stage."
