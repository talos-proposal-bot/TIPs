## Abstract

The Following TIP proposes that Talos allocate ETH from its treasury to the GM ETH (WETH-USDC) pool on GMX V2, a high-performing, deep-liquidity ETH/USDC pool. This strategic move is aimed at maximizing yield on idle ETH while supporting one of the most established and innovative protocols in the Arbitrum ecosystem.

GMX has proven itself as a category leader in Perp Dex’s over the past four years, excelling in adoption, liquidity depth,  and revenue generation. With over $482M in TVL and more than $320 billion in trading volume to date, GMX is one of the most trusted and widely used on-chain trading platforms.

Offering an 18.94% APR and backed by $101M in TVL, the GMETH (WETH-USDC) pool combines strong returns with deep liquidity making it an attractive opportunity for Treasury allocation.

By deploying ETH into GMETH, Talos can put its treasury assets to productive use, earning passive yield while gaining exposure to a battle-tested, revenue-generating DeFi primitive. This proposal aligns with Talos’s goals of responsible treasury management and long-term ecosystem alignment.

## Motivation

The GM ETH (WETH-USDC) pool offers a compelling opportunity for Talos to put its treasury assets to work in a smart, sustainable way. GM ETH’s low-beta nature makes it especially well-suited for treasury management, providing steady exposure to ETH with reduced volatility ideal for balancing yield with risk.

Talos' treasury underpins its autonomy and resilience. A core objective is to hold assets that are transparent, deep in liquidity, and functionally useful for liquidity management. With over $554.83 million worth of GM ETH (WETH-USDC)  has been minted on Arbitrum, backed by more than 4,695 liquidity providers. On top of that, the current APR of 13.61% (at the time of writing) represents a very attractive yield, especially for an asset with deep & stable liquidity.



## Specification

Asset: ETH

Amount: The treasury may initiate a deposit and strategically scale it over time based on performance and market conditions

Pool: GMETH (WETH-USDc)

Token Received: GMETH/USDC (By depositing into this GM pool, you get 50% exposure to ETH and 50% exposure to USDC giving you a balanced position)

Lockup Period: None (position can be exited anytime)

## Rationale

- The GM ETH pool current APR of 13.61%, with last year returns up to 18.94%, offering a compelling passive income opportunity on idle ETH.

- GMX has been live on Arbitrum since its inception, with strong track record, adoption, and TVL metrics.

- GM ETH is a highly liquid, composable asset used across leading DeFi protocols such as Morpho, Dolomite, Venus, and Delta Prime.

- The GMETH pool provides low-beta ETH exposure and is backed by robust Chainlink oracle pricing for reliable asset valuation.

- Supporting GMX strengthens Talos’s relationships within the Arbitrum ecosystem and supports long-term ecosystem growth.


## Security Considerations

The GMX protocol, including assets like gmETH, is built on smart contracts that have been professionally audited multiple times by Guardian, which helps enhance user trust and minimize potential code vulnerabilities. The protocol also has an active Immunefi bug bounty program. The prices you see for gmETH come from secure, decentralized oracles like Chainlink and Chaos Labs, helping to ensure they’re fair and not easily manipulated. While GMX works hard to keep things secure, it’s still a good idea for users to be aware of the usual risks that come with DeFi protocols. The GMX contributors are always closely monitoring the system, fixing issues, and improving security in collaboration with security providers. In addition to this the decisions about security and updates are made together with the Security Committee and DAO considerations, so everyone’s interests are taken into account.

## Implementation

Allocating ETH to the GM ETH pool on GMX V2 offers Talos a smart, yield-generating use of treasury assets while strengthening ties to a core protocol in the Arbitrum ecosystem. This proposal balances risk and return, supports long-term growth for the ecosystem, and aligns with Talos’s mission of efficient and strategic treasury management.
