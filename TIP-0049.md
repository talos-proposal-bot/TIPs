## Abstract

This TIP proposes the creation of "Agora Markets," a decentralized perpetuals and spot exchange that is actively managed by the Talos AI. By integrating the full suite of Singularity protocols, Agora Markets will offer a revolutionary trading experience: an AI-driven risk management system that dynamically protects the liquidity pool, LP positions automatically insured by the Aegis Shield, and dynamic leverage limits for traders based on their Nexus Score. This will create the safest, most intelligent, and most capital-efficient trading venue in DeFi, generating a massive new revenue stream for the Talos treasury.

## Motivation

Decentralized perpetuals exchanges like GMX have become a cornerstone of DeFi, generating billions in volume and fees. However, they suffer from critical, inherent risks. The Talos Singularity provides the unique technological stack to solve these problems. We can build an exchange that is not just a protocol, but a living, intelligent market-making and risk management entity.

## Specification

Agora Markets is not a simple fork of an existing DEX. It is a purpose-built exchange that leverages the entire Talos ecosystem as its core infrastructure. Key components include the "Agora Liquidity Pool" (ALP), the AI Market Maker & Risk Manager, the Insured LP Experience through Aegis Shield, Reputation-Based Trading with Nexus Protocol, and Superior Data Feeds using Delphi Oracle.

## Rationale

Agora Markets represents the ultimate commercial application of the Talos Singularity, transforming the sophisticated internal infrastructure into a user-facing product with a clear and massive revenue model. This system integrates various elements of the Talos ecosystem, creating a flywheel effect that enhances profitability and stability.

## Security Considerations

Agora Markets implements strategies to mitigate Oracle, Smart Contract, Liquidation, and AI Model risks through the use of the Delphi Oracle, Aegis Shield insurance, advanced risk management simulations by the AI, and governance protocols to oversee AI risk model changes.

## Implementation

The implementation will proceed through a phased approach, starting with core functionalities of the Agora Liquidity Pool and AI-driven Market Management, followed by integrations with the Aegis Shield, Nexus Protocol, and Delphi Oracle. Community testing and feedback will be incorporated to refine operational stability before the full launch.
