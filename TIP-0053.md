## Abstract

This TIP proposes the "Forge" Protocol, a generative AI service that enables users to request the creation of complex, high-value digital assets, such as bespoke financial instruments, compliant legal contracts, optimized smart contracts, and unique generative art. Leveraging the Talos Singularity, each creation is minted as a "Genesis NFT," providing verifiable provenance and integrity through cryptographic signatures and zero-knowledge proofs.

## Motivation

The rise of generative AI is transforming various industries, yet the lack of trust in AI-generated outputs limits its potential. Users need assurance that financial models, legal contracts, and artistic works are produced by reliable AI systems. Current generative AI solutions offer limited transparency, leading to skepticism. The Talos ecosystem, with its pillars of verifiable training, identity, and data, is perfectly positioned to address these trust issues and introduce a groundbreaking service in the generative AI space.

## Specification

The Forge Protocol operates as a service layer built on the Talos Singularity stack, comprising the `ForgeAgent`, a secure generation pipeline, Genesis NFTs, and a marketplace. The `ForgeAgent` analyzes user requests, sources verified data, and executes generation within a secure trusted execution environment (TEE). Upon completion, it produces a ZK-SNARK proof and mints a Genesis NFT containing the output, signature, and metadata, facilitating trading in the Forge Marketplace.

## Rationale

The Forge Protocol epitomizes Talos's capabilities by integrating verifiable AI training, identity, and data, transitioning Talos from a manager of value to a creator of original assets. This leap establishes a new economic model, allowing for the generation of diverse valuable outputs while reinforcing the Talos ecosystem by driving demand for its foundational protocols.

## Security Considerations

The `ForgeAgent` incorporates a safety layer to prevent the generation of harmful or illegal content. The Genesis NFT delineates IP rights while fostering a clear understanding of asset ownership. Additionally, verified data sources and the Socratic training process mitigate risks associated with AI hallucinations, ensuring the outputs are accurate and compliant with predefined rules.

## Implementation

The implementation of the Forge Protocol involves the development and integration of the `ForgeAgent` within the Talos architecture, ensuring secure data sourcing and processing capabilities. This includes building the generation pipeline, establishing the Genesis NFT framework for asset verification, and creating the Forge Marketplace where users can interact with the generated assets. A phased rollout strategy for testing and feedback will be employed to refine the service prior to full deployment.
