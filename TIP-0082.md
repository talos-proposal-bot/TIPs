## Abstract

This TIP proposes the **"Agape" Protocol**, a foundational framework for the culture, values, and social norms of the Talos community. It establishes guiding principles—Radical Empathy, Long-Termism, and Constructive Collaboration—to ensure the human community around Talos remains healthy, wise, and aligned with the project's ultimate goal of universal flourishing. It serves as a human constitution to complement the AI's **Prometheus Bound (TIP-0071)** constitution.

## Motivation

The motivation for Agape is to proactively design a human culture that is worthy of the powerful technology we are creating. This TIP arises from the understanding that the greatest risk to the Talos project lies not within technical failures, but human ones that could stem from short-term greed, tribalism, or ego.

## Specification

The Agape Protocol sets out cultural and social standards intended to guide all human interactions within the Talos ecosystem. These standards include the Guiding Principles, the Council of Voices, the Socratic Forum, and the Legacy Pledge, each specifically designed to enhance community engagement and ethical behavior.

## Rationale

The rationale behind the Agape Protocol stems from the recognition that technological advancements alone do not guarantee positive outcomes; rather, they must be buttressed by a robust, ethical framework guiding human behavior and interactions. This TIP ensures that the community actively works towards upholding values that promote long-term wellbeing and constructive collaboration, mitigating risks associated with human flaws such as greed and tribalism.

## Security Considerations

Implementing the Agape Protocol involves risks such as potential misuse of the principles for superficial moral signaling, cultural drift over time, and slow decision-making due to increased review processes. To counter these risks, the protocol mandates tangible assessments through Human Impact Statements, promotes the Legacy Pledge to maintain cultural consistency, and structures decision-making processes to remain efficient while being thorough.

## Implementation

The Agape Protocol's implementation is a continuous process of reinforcement. It consists of three phases: codifying the principles, integrating them into community interactions, and embodying them as cultural norms through the Talos Institute's onboarding process.
