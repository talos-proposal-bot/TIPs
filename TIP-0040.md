## Abstract

This TIP proposes "Project Chimera," a multi-stage evolution to transform the Talos AI into a truly intelligent, transparent, and ecosystem-integrated autonomous agent. It introduces three synergistic modules: the "Oracle" Intelligence Layer, the "Rationale" Transparency Mandate, and the "Sentinel" Security & Yield Mandate.

## Motivation

The current Talos model is a groundbreaking first step in autonomous protocol ownership. To maximize its potential, the AI must evolve beyond a "black box" that only manages yield. Project Chimera addresses the need for smarter decision-making, transparency in reasoning, and active contributions to network security.

## Specification

Project Chimera will be implemented in three interconnected modules: the "Oracle" Intelligence Layer, the "Rationale" Transparency Mandate, and the "Sentinel" Security & Yield Mandate, each enhancing a core capability of the Talos AI and creating a self-reinforcing loop that improves decision-making and builds community trust.

## Rationale

These modules collectively transform Talos from a reactive treasury manager into a proactive and ecosystem-aware entity, enhancing intelligence through community input, transparent reasoning, and diversifying yield while contributing to the economic security of the Arbitrum ecosystem.

## Security Considerations

It is crucial to ensure that the integration of community feedback and decision-making processes maintains the security of the AI. Implementing robust mechanisms for reporting and transparency will mitigate risks associated with potential exploitations of the AI's autonomy during its operational phases.

## Implementation

The implementation will unfold in three phases: Phase 1 focuses on the Rationale module's XAI reporting system; Phase 2 develops the Oracle's prediction market; and Phase 3 expands the Sentinel's restaking capabilities, ensuring a structured rollout of improvements to the Talos protocol.
