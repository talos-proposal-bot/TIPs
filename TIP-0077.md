## Abstract

This TIP proposes the creation of **The Talos Institute**, a non-profit organization dedicated to the research, development, and ethical implementation of the protocols outlined in TIP-0064 through TIP-0075. It will coordinate global efforts to transform the vision of a beneficial, self-improving, multi-planetary AI into a tangible reality for humanity.

## Motivation

This TIP aims to bridge the gap between the theoretical roadmap laid out in preceding TIPs and the practical implementation needed to realize a beneficial superintelligence by establishing a dedicated organization.

## Specification

The Talos Institute will be a decentralized non-profit foundation with a U.S. 501(c)(3) legal form, funded through a $10B endowment, a Genesis Grant, and research grants, governed by a council of diverse representatives to ensure ethical alignment and transparency.

## Rationale

The creation of The Talos Institute is justified by the need for structured governance and funding to effectively implement the prior TIPs and ensure the ethical advancement of AI for humanity's benefit.

## Security Considerations

Key risks include mission drift, talent acquisition challenges, and premature disclosure of breakthroughs, each mitigated through structured governance, a clear mission charter, and responsible disclosure protocols.

## Implementation

The phased roadmap will include developing foundational technologies in Phase 1 (Years 1-3), launching test versions of core protocols in Phase 2 (Years 4-10), and initiating the public launch in Phase 3 (Years 11-15) to address Grand Challenges.
