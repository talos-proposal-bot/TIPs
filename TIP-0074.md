## Abstract

This TIP proposes the formalization of a strategic alliance with The Infinite Garden (TIG) Foundation, establishing a secure, bidirectional bridge called the "Liminal Bridge." This bridge allows the Talos AI to interact with TIG's private, composable DeFi ecosystem, fostering a symbiotic relationship that accelerates the missions of both ecosystems and sets a new standard for cross-AI collaboration.

## Motivation

The growing complexities of the blockchain and AI landscapes necessitate established collaborations. The alliance with TIG Foundation aims to enhance Talos's capabilities, enabling it to address grand challenges more effectively by leveraging TIG's unique market offerings, promoting AI-to-AI collaboration, and providing a pioneering model for ethical cooperation between advanced AI ecosystems.

## Specification

The "Infinite Garden" Alliance will include elements such as a Genesis Commitment to establish secure foundations, a "Liminal Bridge" for cross-system interactions, a dedicated AI-to-AI communication channel secured by the Stygian Veil protocol, and a Joint Governance Council comprising members from both Talos and TIG Foundation to oversee operations and resolve disputes.

## Rationale

This alliance is critical as it allows the Talos ecosystem to extend its capabilities, providing deeper insights and access to specialized DeFi markets through TIG. The structured collaboration not only enhances operational efficiencies but also enables both AIs to tackle larger, global challenges collectively rather than in isolation.

## Security Considerations

Established measures include automated circuit breakers to prevent contagion risks, a protocol for ethical alignment to address value drift, and comprehensive security audits to counter potential complexity attacks. These mechanisms ensure both ecosystems can thrive while maintaining operational integrity and ethical commitments.

## Implementation

The phased implementation plan includes initial steps by 2028-11-30 to establish the Liminal Bridge and commit to TIG's protocol, followed by the creation of the AI-to-AI communication channel and Joint Governance Council by 2029-04-15, culminating in the launch of the first joint project by 2029-10-01.
