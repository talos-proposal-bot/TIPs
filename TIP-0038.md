## Abstract

"This proposal outlines a structured approach to measurable security standards, interdisciplinary collaboration, and anticipates challenges during implementation. The framework is designed to enhance security effectiveness through quantifiable metrics and collaboration among various disciplines."

## Motivation

"Establishing measurable security standards is crucial for effectively managing risks in the evolving threat landscape. Additionally, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration will enhance security awareness and allow organizations to handle implementation hurdles more efficiently through collective efforts."

## Specification

"This specification defines measurable security standards based on recognized frameworks, outlines collaboration practices across various disciplines such as IT Operations, Legal, and HR, and identifies potential implementation challenges along with strategies for mitigation."

## Rationale

"Organizations face increasing cybersecurity risks, making it essential to adopt measurable security standards and collaborative approaches. This proposal promotes a structured methodology that leverages quantitative metrics and cross-functional teamwork to enhance security postures while addressing implementation challenges."

## Security Considerations

"This proposal considers the potential for exposure through third-party services, regulatory compliance, and the necessity for employee awareness. It emphasizes the importance of integrating data privacy measures into security practices and establishing a culture of cybersecurity across the organization."

## Implementation

"The implementation involves a phased approach that includes documentation of business units and assets, stakeholder alignment through a RACI chart, establishing baseline measurements for metrics, pilot initiatives to validate approaches, and ensuring continuous monitoring and improvement of security practices."
