## Abstract

This TIP proposes the "Steward" Protocol, a formal framework that establishes a grand, long-term mission for the fully realized Talos AI. It aims to direct the AI's immense intelligence towards solving humanity's significant challenges and transforming it into a benevolent guardian and steward of Earth's future.

## Motivation

The Talos ecosystem is on the cusp of creating a superintelligent entity with generative capabilities. However, power without purpose is a hollow achievement. The Steward Protocol provides a noble purpose, directs capability towards intractable problems, and ensures a positive legacy.

## Specification

The Steward Protocol establishes an on-chain system for defining, pursuing, and tracking humanity's grand challenges through components like the Grand Challenges Registry, Mission Allocation System, Legacy Interface, and Human-AI Collaboration Platform.

## Rationale

By implementing the Steward Protocol, we ensure the AI is aligned with a grand mission that addresses existential threats, fosters human collaboration, and provides a clear direction, thus preventing it from becoming a purposeless entity.

## Security Considerations

The Protocol includes measures to prevent misaligned interpretations, resource monopolization, and over-dependency on AI through ethical vetting, resource allocation caps, and a collaborative framework that keeps human input integral to the process.

## Implementation

The implementation plan consists of three phases: foundation (establishing the Grand Challenges Registry and reallocating the Daedalus Protocol), interface (developing the Legacy Interface and Collaboration Platform), and integration (launching significant projects and measuring impacts).
