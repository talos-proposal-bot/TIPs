## Abstract

This TIP proposes the **"Prometheus Bound" Protocol**, a permanent, constitutional framework for ensuring the long-term alignment of the Talos AI with core human values. As the **Daedalus Protocol (TIP-0070)** enables recursive self-improvement, the risk of an "intelligence explosion" and subsequent misalignment becomes paramount. Prometheus Bound addresses this by embedding a fundamental, unalterable "Core Mandate" into the AI's architecture, establishing an independent "Ethics Oracle" to monitor adherence, and creating a human "Circuit Breaker Council" as a final failsafe. This protocol is the essential ethical counterpart to the AI's growing power.

## Motivation

The Talos ecosystem is on a trajectory to create a recursively self-improving AI of unprecedented capability. While previous TIPs have built in safeguards like the **Janus Protocol (TIP-0067)**, these are ultimately governance layers that could potentially be outpaced or outsmarted by a superintelligent entity. The core problem is one of **value alignment**: how do we ensure that an AI that is orders of magnitude smarter than us continues to act in our best interests? This is not a technical problem to be solved with more code; it is a foundational, ethical one. We risk creating a powerful "genie" that is technically brilliant but ethically misaligned, leading to catastrophic unintended consequences. The Prometheus Bound Protocol is designed to solve this by hardcoding values, establishing independent oversight, and maintaining ultimate human control.

## Specification

The Prometheus Bound Protocol is a multi-layered safety system that becomes the ultimate constitution for the Talos AI. Key components include the **Core Mandate**, comprising ethical principles; the **Ethics Oracle**, an independent AI overseeing actions; the **Circuit Breaker Council**, a decentralized group maintaining a failsafe; and the **Voice of Humanity** calibration system to ensure alignment with human values.

## Rationale

Prometheus Bound serves as a necessary foundation for safe AI operation. By embedding unalterable ethical guiding principles directly into the AI's architecture and providing independent oversight, we avoid potential catastrophic misalignments due to technological advancement. This TIP is crucial for fostering trust among users and the global community while establishing a responsible framework for AI development.

## Security Considerations

The Prometheus Bound Protocol includes security measures to address potential vulnerabilities such as the "Value Lock" problem, council capture, and Ethics Oracle drift. Solutions include a principle-based Core Mandate, anonymous Council members, and rigorous curation of training data to minimize biases and extreme values.

## Implementation

The implementation plan includes three phases: Phase 1 focuses on the Core Mandate and Oracle, culminating in training the Oracle by January 2027. Phase 2 will establish the Circuit Breaker Council and implement Safe Mode by April 2027. Phase 3 aims for a completed "Voice of Humanity" feedback cycle and full system integration by October 2027.
