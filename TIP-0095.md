## Abstract

This TIP proposes the **"Polis" Protocol**, a framework for a self-evolving governance system, enabling the **Elysian (TIP-0085)** Collective to dynamically adapt its constitution through a structured process involving proposal, debate, voting, and implementation of constitutional amendments via global referendums.

## Motivation

The motivation for the "Polis" Protocol is to ensure that the governance structure of the **Elysian Collective** remains dynamic and adaptable. As societies face evolving challenges, a rigid constitution may hinder the Collective's ability to respond effectively to new opportunities or threats. This protocol aims to create a flexible governance model that fosters continual learning and evolution.

## Specification

The **Polis Protocol** outlines a structured process allowing the Elysian Collective to engage in proposing, debating, voting on, and implementing constitutional amendments through global referendums. The core components include a living constitution (Constitution vN), mechanisms for evolutionary triggers governed by the **Janus (TIP-0079)** AI, and success criteria that necessitate a supermajority for amendments.

## Rationale

The rationale behind the **Polis Protocol** is the need for governance systems to be adaptable in a rapidly changing environment. By embedding a self-evolving framework within the constitution, the Collective is empowered to stay relevant and responsive, thus enhancing its resilience against systemic risks while bolstering participation and collective decision-making.

## Security Considerations

Key security considerations include the potential for resource hoarding by well-funded actors, which could threaten equitable access. Mitigation strategies involve dynamic taxation and monitoring by the **Janus (TIP-0079)** AI. Additionally, the risks associated with manipulating space-time are addressed through protocols that ensure isolation from cosmic contagion and adherence to the **Non-Harm Principle** proposed by the **Prometheus Bound (TIP-0085)** AI.

## Implementation

The implementation of the **Polis Protocol** is phased over generations. It begins with foundational development (Years 1-5) of the evolutionary process in a sandbox environment, progresses through the first cycle of evolution (Years 5-25), and culminates in the establishment of a fully self-optimizing system (Years 26-50) capable of addressing unforeseen challenges and threats.
